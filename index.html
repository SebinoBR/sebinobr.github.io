About Me

I am a digital creator exploring the overlaps between software engineering, music production, graphic design. My work explores the darker aesthetics of the digital realm, embracing trap metal sounds, hyperrealistic renders, and experimental interfaces.

My trap metal productions draw inspiration from boundary-pushing artists such as Scarlxrd and Ghostemane, reimagining their melodic sensibilities in my own way.

My products tend to be a fusion of hyperrealism and dreamlike elements, created in rapid production cycles that embrace spontaneity and allow me to stay prolific.

When I'm not creating or learning (strongly tied synonyms), you can probably find me in the dojo, in solitude, working on something.

My professional journey has so far led me deep into the world of software development, where I've found a particular fascination with DevOps ecosystems, the automation of deployment pipelines and infrastructure as code. The elegant choreography between development and operations resonates with my appreciation for systems that very feasibly provides technical precision and improves efficiency all across the board.

The ephemeral nature of cloud infrastructure fascinates me – how systems can materialize and dissolve on demand. This makes me feel as if my technical work is infused with a sense of urgency, precision and possibility that pushes me to refine my craft continuously.
